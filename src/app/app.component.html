To Do List App

This is a TodoList-App developed in Angular with Bootstrap for the desing and others libraries like SweetAlert, FontAwesome and Material Icons.
It is a single page application, which consists of creating a list of things to do, which you can edit or delete according to your needs. These tasks will remain saved in your browser even after a page reload or until they are manually deleted.
